OUR HERITAGE

Born and brewed in Southern California since 1963, The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf® has always been passionate about connecting loyal customers with carefully handcrafted products.

CARING CUP

At The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf®, we believe it is not enough to do well—we must do good. Our social responsibility efforts all over the world, through our Caring Cup program, blend commitment and community. We respond to the needs of local communities served by our stores and the needs of communities where our products are grown.

BE ONE OF US

Our passion for being Simply The Best™ in everything we do goes way deeper than the bottom of the cup. It’s really rooted in people.
